Ranking the Greatest Lightsaber Duels in Star Wars

Posted on Nov 20, 2025

Lightsaber duels are the heart of Star Wars. But which battles are truly the best? Let’s rank them by choreography, stakes, and emotional weight.

The Criteria

Great lightsaber duels need:

  • Technical choreography - Exciting to watch
  • Emotional stakes - We care about the outcome
  • Story significance - Advances character or plot
  • Visual spectacle - Memorable imagery

The Worst Duel

Finn vs. Riot Trooper - The Force Awakens

This isn’t a lightsaber duel, but it’s hilariously bad. A random stormtrooper with a shock baton holds his own against a lightsaber? Come on.

What Makes a Great Duel?

The best lightsaber battles combine:

  1. Emotional stakes > Flashy choreography
  2. Character development > Cool moves
  3. Story significance > Spectacle
  4. Meaning > Length

Notice: The top duels aren’t the longest or most acrobatic - they’re the most meaningful.

Conclusion

Lightsaber duels work best when they’re about MORE than fighting. The blade clash is just the medium for the real conflict - ideological, emotional, or moral.

That’s why Mustafar still reigns supreme. It’s not just a fight - it’s the death of a friendship and the birth of a monster.
